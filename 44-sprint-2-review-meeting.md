## What's in a Name?

5 mins.

A product name is important because it shapes how people first perceive and engage with what you’ve created.

Take a few minutes as a team to decide on three candidate names for your product to suggest to the client.

The Software Sustainability Institute’s [guide on naming projects and products](https://www.software.ac.uk/guide/choosing-project-and-product-names) may provide some helpful pointers.

:::

## Sprint 2 Review

Following our second sprint, as before, we now prepare for and hold a Sprint Review meeting for the client.
You may find it useful to refer back to the [material on sprint reviews](32-sprint-1-review-meeting.md).

:::challenge

## Prepare for the Sprint Review

Take 30 minutes to prepare for the Sprint 2 Review.

- Prepare your demonstration and decide who will present each element
- Write a structure (or agenda) for the meeting (including a short item on deciding a product name from the candidates)
- Consider how your progress has moved the project toward the Product Goal
- Prepare any questions you have for the client
- Decide who will take on each role

:::


## Sprint 2 Review Meeting

Teams conduct the Sprint Review meeting with the clients (30 mins).


## Update Product Backlog

Teams spend 15 minutes updating the Product Backlog (15 mins).
