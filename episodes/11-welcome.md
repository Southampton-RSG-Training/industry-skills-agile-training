:::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::  challenge

## Group Exercise: Introductions and Deciding a Team Name

15 mins.

In your groups:

- (Maximum of 2 mins each) Have each of you briefly introduce yourselves and list your development skills, noting them in your team GoogleDoc
- (Maximum of 5 mins) As a group, discuss and decide on a team name that reflects your group skills and values

Ensure you keep to time!

::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::
